Ghana

Ghana is a democratic republic in West Africa, located on the Gulf of Guinea, and the first sub-Saharan African nation to gain independence in 1957. With diverse landscapes including coastal plains, tropical rainforests, and savannah, it boasts the world's largest artificial lake, Lake Volta. The nation maintains a multi-party system, has significant freedom of speech and the press, and its official language is English, though many indigenous languages are also spoken.
